The story

Born in a
pandemic.
Built on love.

"When the world stopped, I started cooking for the people around me. I didn't want to stop."

Mumbai Adda was born during COVID-19. Blossom started cooking from home — real Mumbai recipes, made the way her city taught her. Rich curries, fiery Indo-Chinese, the Vada Pav her neighbourhood runs on.

What began as feeding neighbours became something bigger. Sutton's adda — a gathering spot, a community kitchen, a little piece of Mumbai in South London.
